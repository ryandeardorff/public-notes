> [[MAT 200 Hub - Calc II|MAT 200 Hub - Calc II]], [[Integration by Parts|Integration by Parts]]

If we perform integration by parts and get the same integral as our $\int v\, du$
This can be good!

$$
\int 5x \, dx = \dots - \#\int 5x\, dx
$$

That's like saying:
$$
y = \dots - \#y
$$
We can solve that!
