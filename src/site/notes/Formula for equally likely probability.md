> [[MAT 258 Hub - Discrete Mathematics|MAT 258 Hub - Discrete Mathematics]]

$$
P(E) = \frac{|E|}{|\Omega|}
$$
where $|E| =$ size of $E$
$|\Omega|$ = size of $\Omega$

**Use this formula ONLY if the outcomes in $\Omega$ are equally likely**
