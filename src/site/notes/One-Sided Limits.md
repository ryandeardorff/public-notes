[[The Limit|The two sided limit]] 
$$\lim_{x\rightarrow a} f(x)$$ exists only when both one-sided limits agree:
$$
\lim_{x\rightarrow a}f(x) = \lim_{x\rightarrow a^{+}} f(x) = \lim_{x\rightarrow a^{-}} f(x)
$$
