#math 
>[[MAT 150 Hub - Calc I|MAT 150 Hub - Calc I]]

$$
\tan(x) = \frac{\sin(x)}{\cos(x)}
$$
$$
\sec(x) = \frac{1}{\cos(x)}
$$
$$
\cot(x) = \frac{\cos(x)}{\sin(x)}
$$
$$
\csc(x) = \frac{1}{\sin(x)}
$$
