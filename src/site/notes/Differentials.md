In [[The Derivative#^1e94d4|Leibniz Notation]] we see $\frac{dy}{dx}$ This is a differential

We can use this in equations:
$$
\begin{align}
\frac{d}{dx}y = \frac{dy}{dx}&=2x \\
\\
dy&=2xdx 
\\ \\
&\text{or} \\
de^{2x} = e^{2x} d2x &= 2e^{2x}dx
\end{align}
$$
