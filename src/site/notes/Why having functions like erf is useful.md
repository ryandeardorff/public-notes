$$
\begin{align}
&erf(x) \\
&\rightarrow erf(0) = 0\\
&\rightarrow erf'(x) = \frac{2}{\pi} e^{-x^{2}}\\
\end{align}
$$

$$
\begin{align}
&\frac{d}{dx}erf(x) = \frac{d}{dx}erf(x) + x \frac{d}{dx}erf(x) = erf(x) + x\left(\frac{2}{\sqrt{\pi}} e ^{-x^{2}}\right) \\
\end{align}
$$

This kind of stuff lets us solve and simplify other problems.
