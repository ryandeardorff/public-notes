a) 
$$
\begin{align*}
P(F) &= P(M)P(F|M) + P(M^{C}) P(F|M^{C})\\
&= (0.7)(0.8) + (0.3)(0.4)\\
&= 0.68
\end{align*}
$$

b)
Given $P(F|M) = 0.8$
Find $P(M|F)=?$

We're going to use [[Baye's Formula|Baye's Formula]]
$$
P(M|F) = \frac{P(M\cap F)}{P(F)} = \frac{(0.7)(0.8)}{0.68 \textcolor{gray}{\leftarrow \text{answer from (a)}}} = \frac{56}{68}
$$
