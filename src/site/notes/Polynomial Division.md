It's long division for polynomials
$$
\begin{align}
x+2& \leftarrow \text{quotient} \\
x-3 \,\overline{\smash{)}x^{2}- x + 4}& \\
x^{2}-3x\qquad& \\
\overline{\qquad2x}+4& \\
2x-6& \\
\overline{\qquad10}&\leftarrow \text{remainder}
\end{align}
$$

$$
\frac{x^{2}-x+4}{x-3} = x+2 + \frac{10}{x-3}
