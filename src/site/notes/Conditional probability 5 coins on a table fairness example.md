5 coins sit on a table: 4 are fair, 1 is trick ($H$ both sides)
You pick a coin and flip it 4 times and get $H$ every time.

What is the probability you picked the trick coin?

$T=$ pick trick coin
$A=$ get 4 heads in 4 flips

Find $P(T|A)$
