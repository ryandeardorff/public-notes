> [[MAT 150 Hub - Calc I|MAT 150 Hub - Calc I]]

$$
|x| = \begin{cases}  
x &x \ge 0\\
-x &x < 0\\
\end{cases}
$$
It's where we split up a function, could be:
$$
f(x) = \begin{cases} 
x+5 & x > 5\\
x^{2} & x \le 5\\
\end{cases}
$$
