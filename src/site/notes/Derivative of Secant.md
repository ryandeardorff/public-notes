>[[MAT 150 Hub - Calc I|MAT 150 Hub - Calc I]], [[Tangent, Secant, Cotangent, and Cosecant|Tangent, Secant, Cotangent, and Cosecant]]

$$
\frac{d}{dx}\sec{x} = \frac{\sin{x}}{cos^{2}x} = \sec{x}\tan{x}
$$
