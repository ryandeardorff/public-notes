> [[MAT 258 Hub - Discrete Mathematics|MAT 258 Hub - Discrete Mathematics]], [[The Floor Function|The Floor Function]]

$$
\lceil x\rceil = ceil(x) = \text{round up }x \text{ to nearest integer}
$$

$$
\begin{align*}
\lceil2.1\rceil &= 3\\
\lceil-2.1\rceil &= -2\\
\lceil2\rceil &= 2\\
\lceil-2\rceil &= -2\\
\end{align*}
$$
