> [[MAT 258 Hub - Discrete Mathematics|MAT 258 Hub - Discrete Mathematics]], [[Quantifiers|Quantifiers]]

If $\forall x\, P(x)$ is T, then $\exists x \, P(x)$ is T.
I.e. $\forall x \, P(x) \rightarrow \exists x \, P(x)$ is a [[Tautology|Tautology]].
